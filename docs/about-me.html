DOMI · THE MIND BEHIND CAPYTAROT

I’m Domi, late 30s — with 20 years of experience across magic, stagecraft, and research.

I work as a medium, magician, and showman — and also as a physicist, consciousness researcher, and practical occultist. To me, these aren’t contradictions. They are different languages for the same mission: read patterns, understand timing, and turn insight into real movement.

When I’m not building rituals, readings, or concepts for Capytarot, you’ll find me skateboarding or skydiving. That is where I train the same core I use in my work: focus, presence, and trust under pressure.

What defines my work

  • 20 years of practice: stage, ritual work, coaching, and real-life application.
  • Bridge-builder: spirituality, performance, psychology, and physics in one clear language.
  • Clear over vague: no foggy talk — concrete impulses that actually help.
  • Capytarot principle: depth can stay light; humor and precision can coexist.

Capytarot is my lab for modern mysticism: grounded, creative, and practical. If you want to dive deeper, jump back to the main page and get your reading.
